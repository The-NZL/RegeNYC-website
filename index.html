FAQs

What is orthobiologic medicine?

A field using the body’s natural healing processes to repair musculoskeletal injuries.

Is regenerative therapy safe?

Yes, especially when performed by trained professionals using your own biological materials.

How long is recovery after PRP?

Most patients resume normal activity within days and experience results in 4–6 weeks.
